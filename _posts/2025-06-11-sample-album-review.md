## How to create a new post

Create a new file in `_posts/` with this naming format:

**YYYY-MM-DD-your-title-here.md**

## Front matter options

Here's what you can add at the top of each post:

- **title** - Your post title
- **date** - The date (YYYY-MM-DD)
- **tags** - Categories like album spotlight, review, etc.
- **image** - Hero image at the top (album art URL or your own photo)
- **spotify** - Embed a full album, track, or playlist (paste the Spotify link)
- **sticky_track** - A track that sticks to the bottom of the page while reading (paste a Spotify track link)
- **vinyl_photo** - A photo of your vinyl (put images in `/assets/img/vinyl/`)
- **vinyl_caption** - Caption under the vinyl photo

## That's it

Just write your thoughts in regular text below the front matter. You can use **bold**, *italic*, and all the usual markdown formatting.
